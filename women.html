What Our Website Does

  • Welcome to Suraksha360 – Empowering Women’s Safety
  • At the heart of Suraksha360 is a simple yet powerful concept: two rubber bangles that act as safety devices. These bangles are equipped with embedded sensors that can detect when they are separated. The moment the bangles are distanced, an alert is sent to the nearest police station or emergency contact, notifying them of the danger.
  • Along with real-time alerts, the website provides a user-friendly dashboard to manage settings, contacts, and emergency alerts.
  • We prioritize your safety by integrating our system with emergency response teams, making your well-being our top priority.

How to Use the Website

  1. Welcome to Suraksha360 – Empowering Women’s Safety
  2. Sign up to Suraksha360 using your email or mobile number.
  3. Register all your information including your name, emergency contact, and location details.
  4. Click on the Settings button.
  5. Enable all necessary permissions such as location, Bluetooth, and notifications.
  6. Add up to 3 emergency contact numbers who will receive alerts in case of danger.
  7. Connect your Suraksha360 bangles via Bluetooth if applicable.
  8. Customize alert preferences like SOS tone, vibration, or silent mode.
  9. You are Good to go!!

About Us

  • Suraksha360 is an innovative women’s safety solution built to empower and protect women through smart wearable technology.
  • The system uses rubber bangles embedded with separation sensors.
  • When the bangles are separated during an emergency, a distress alert is instantly sent to emergency contacts or nearby authorities.
  • Our aim is to create a safe, responsive, and user-friendly support system for real-time emergency response.
  • Suraksha360 is not just a tool—it’s a movement for safety, dignity, and freedom for every woman.
